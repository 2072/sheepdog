A very simple add-on that should prevent you from breaking crowd control spell.

**Implemented Features:**

- Bark when you are targeting a crowd controlled unit.
*Actually I would need a good quality (free) dog barking sound...*

**Planed features:**

- Warn you gently when a unit has been crowd controlled somewhere nearby.

- Should be able to disable itself depending on the player role


*Currently supported crowd controlling spells:*

- Banish
- Freezing Trap Effect
- Hibernate
- Polymorph
- Sap
- Seduction
- Shackle Undead
